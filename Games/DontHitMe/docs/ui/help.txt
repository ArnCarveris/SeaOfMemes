F1 to remove help.

M - toggle map mode.
G - cycle grid styles.
+/= - increase speed
- - decrease speed

ESC to toggle arrow cursor/cross cursor.

W,S,A,D - move forward, back, left, right.
up arrow, down arrow - move forward, back.
left arrow, right arrow - turn left/right.

PageUp/PageDown - move up, down.
' ' and 'X' - move up, down.

Cross cursor: mouse turns eye.
Arrow cursor: left or right drag turns eye.
